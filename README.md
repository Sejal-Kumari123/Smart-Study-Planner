# 📚 Smart Study Planner

A simple, offline study planner web app to help students organize their subjects, track progress, and stay on schedule.

## ✨ Features

- **Multi-User Login** — Sign up and log in with your own username and password. Each user gets their own private data.
- **Dashboard** — See your overall progress, study streak, motivational quotes, and today's timetable at a glance.
- **Subject Manager** — Add subjects with exam dates and total chapters. Track your progress by marking chapters as completed.
- **Revision Timetable** — Schedule study blocks with start/end times and assign tasks for Today or Tomorrow.
- **Study Timer** — Built-in 25-minute focus timer with Start, Pause, and Reset controls.
- **Urgent Revision Alert** — Automatically warns you when an exam is less than 7 days away.
- **Dark / Light Mode** — Toggle between themes with a single click.
- **Profile Management** — Update your display name and password from the Profile tab.
